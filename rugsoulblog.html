Rug Soul

We are proud to introduce a unique project that merges the worlds of NFTs and traditional craftsmanship. The Rug Soul NFT Collection redefine luxury by bridging the gap between traditional craftsmanship & blockchain technology, rooted in cultural heritage and artisanal excellence.

Our utility-based NFTs offer holders exclusive access to authentic hand-knotted rug designs, created in close collaboration with the master weaver Rug Creation Nepal. Each NFT in the Rug Soul collection is inspired by ancient symbolism, futuristic visions, and intricate storytelling. These aren't just tokens they are visual narratives brought to life through generations of weaving tradition.

Only 369 NFTs are availableand here's the deal: When you grab one, you’ll get 25% off on your Rug Order from Rug Creation Nepal!


As a holder, you gain:

• 25% off on custom orders of premium, pure Nepali hand-knotted rugs.
• Ownership-linked utility, where each NFT corresponds to a real-world Rug with traceable artisan lineage.
• Access to limited-edition releases, early drops, and collector rewards.
• Community perks including DAO participation and future metaverse integrations.
• Support for a social cause: a portion of all proceeds goes to artisans and cultural preservation initiatives.

Each piece in the Rug Soul 369 Collection has its own story, its own energy, and a timeless value combining the authenticity of handmade tradition with the innovation of blockchain technology.
